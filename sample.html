RetroBot is now available to the public!

May 1, 2022

RetroBot can now be added to any Discord server! There are free and paid features.

    Free: Search Yu-Gi-Oh! cards and check if decks are legal for all formats. No cost.
    Affiliate: Host FormatLibrary.com sanctioned ranked play for one (1) format. One time payment of $15 per server.
    Partner: Host tournaments integrated with your Google Drive (uploads .YDK files and metagame info) and Challonge accounts. - One time $40 set up fee, plus a $30 per year subscription to cover maintenance and server costs.
